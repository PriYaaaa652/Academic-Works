Mine Frequent Patterns

A. Unigrams (Single Words)

Identify the most common individual tokens in the chunk.

Counter([token for row in transactions for token in row])

Reveals vocabulary popularity.

B. Co-occurring Pairs

Identify words that appear together in the same comment (Itemsets).

combinations(sorted(set(row)), 2)

Reveals themes and associations.

Interpretation Guide

  • ~ 1.0 Strong Positive Correlation (Rise together)
  • ~ -1.0 Strong Negative Correlation (Diverge)
  • ~ 0.0 No clear relationship

Interpretation & Reflection

Guiding Questions for your Report:

  • Trends: Do specific words peak in middle chunks? (e.g., "Exam" peaking mid-semester?)
  • Coupling: Do two words always appear together? (e.g., "Like" and "Subscribe")
  • Divergence: Does the mood shift? (e.g., "Fun" drops as "Stress" rises)
  • Hypothesis: Propose a "deeper reason" based on real-world context or video content.
